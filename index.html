TechMaster Program Curriculum

Solution Development

Students will learn the fundamental skills of problem-solving and analytical thinking, focusing on real-world challenges and solution development.

Software Engineering

Students will master the essentials of software engineering, including front-end and back-end development, which serves as the technical foundaation of the program that includes (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, UI/UX, Python, Nodes.js SQL and NoSQL).

Business Management

Beyond technical skills, this section teaches students how to manage tech projects and understands the business aspects of tech.

Projects & Capstone

Studentswill apply what they've learned through individual and group projects, culminating in a final capstone project that showcases their expertise.

Internships

The internship component of the program allows students to gain practical, on-the-job experience, working at top tech companies or startups. This will help students transition smoothly into full-time roles after graduation.

Through the program's special guest lectures and networking sessions, students will build connections with industry leaders.
